Coronavirus disease 2019 (C OVID -19) emerged in late [ADDRESS_630036] been

Much research has proven that SARS -CoV -2 cell entry and fusion depends on

Overall Design:  
This is a Phase 3, randomized, double -blind, placebo -controlled, multicenter study to 
evaluate the safety and efficacy of Proxalutamide (GT0918) in adult outpatients 
diagnosed with mild to moderate COVID -19.

evaluate if anti -androgen therapy may effectively prevent progression to the severe 
form of COVID -19 illness by [CONTACT_490041][INVESTIGATOR_490003].

and efficacious therapeutic agent for COVID -19 during the current outbreak. The
