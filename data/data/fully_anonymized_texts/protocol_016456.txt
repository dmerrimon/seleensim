Recently, severa l treatments were developed and approved as ‘non -invasive’ treatments for fat

swelling and bruising that repeats after every treatment, physicians and subjects are still 
searching for additional alternatives in the market.   
Raziel Therapeutics has discovered that a novel synthetic molecule (termed RZL -012) can help 
reduce fat content in human and pi[INVESTIGATOR_14107]. Recent data has led Raziel to the understanding that the 
mechanism of action (MOA) of RZL -[ADDRESS_361633] excess fat tissue at selected anatomical sites.

overweight subjects in two clinical studies and was evaluated for the treatment of fat disorders

thermogenic effect  (a rise of 1 °C in temperature at the injected site compared to the untreated

This study is planned as a single blind , randomized , placebo -controlled,  Phase 2 a, 2-cohort  study 
for the treatment of submental fat.  Eligible subjects will be randomized to receive either RZL -

subjects vs. placebo treated subjects at Day 84 following injection.  
3. Improvement  from baseline in the self -assessment of submental fat using the Face -Q 
satisfaction questionnaire rating score  in RZL -012 treated subjects vs. placebo treated
