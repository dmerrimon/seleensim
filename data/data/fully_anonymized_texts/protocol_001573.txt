2.5. Rationale  
Imaging and staging of prostate cancer is critical for surgical and treatment planning.  
We aim to image patients with suspected metastatic prostate cancer using Gallium- 68 
labeled HBED -CC PSMA (68Ga PSMA -HBED -CC) in order to demonstrate its 
utility.  We plan to utilize this data to obtain further approvals of the 68Ga PSMA -
HBED -CC compound, so that this agent will become widely available for clinical 
imaging in prostate cancer patien ts.

with recurrent prostate cancer8. Their results show ed a detection rate of 50% for
