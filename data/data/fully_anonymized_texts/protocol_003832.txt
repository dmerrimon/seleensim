1 B. Purpose of the study. 
The overall objective of this study is to investigate the role of gut microbes in 
mediating how humans metabolize and excrete the environmental neurotoxicant 
methylmercury (MeHq). Our secondary objectives are: 1) to derive non-invasive tools to 
evaluate an individual's M eHg metabolizing potential, 2 ) to identify dietary supplement 
approaches to enhance an individual's capacity to metabolize and excrete toxic MeHg, 
3) 
to discover symbiotic bacterial strains with probiotic potential. Successful completion 
of 
the study Aims will eventually lead to advanced methodologies to better assess the 
risks 
of Me Hg on human health and identify factors that mitigate this risk.
