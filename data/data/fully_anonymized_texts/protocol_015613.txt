Study Design:
This is a Phase 2, multicenter, randomized, double -blind, placebo- controlled, parallel -group, study in 
pediatric patients (aged â‰¥2 and â‰¤17 years) with Dravet syndrome and Lennox Gastaut syndrome (LGS)

stratified by 2 categories: patients with Dravet syndrome with convulsive seizures and patients with LGS
