Statistical Analysis Plan  Data preparation. We will use SPSS (Version 27.0. Armonk, NY: IBM Corp.) for all variable preparation and statistical analysis. We will compute summary statistics to evaluate the distributions of each study variable (i.e., PSS, FAAQ) and assess potential outliers  Treatment effect on outcome variables. In a series of Analysis of Covariance (ANCOVA) models, we will compare treatment groups (IV: MED vs. MED+HE vs. HE vs. WL) on each 8-week outcome variable (DV: e.g., PSS, FAAQ,), adjusting for baseline value of each corresponding measure (covariate). If the main ANCOVA model is significant, we will post-hoc (least square differences) tests to explore group differences.
