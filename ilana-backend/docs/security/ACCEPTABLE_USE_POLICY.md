## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy establishes guidelines for the appropriate use of Ilana Immersive information systems, networks, and data. The policy protects employees, contractors, customers, and the company from harmful or illegal actions.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees of Ilana Immersive, LLC
- All contractors and temporary workers
- All third parties with access to Ilana systems
- All devices used to access Ilana resources (company-owned or personal)

## 3. Policy Statement

Ilana Immersive information systems and data are provided to support business operations. Users must use these resources responsibly, ethically, and in compliance with applicable laws and company policies.

## 4. General Use Guidelines

### 4.1 Authorized Use

Company systems may be used for:
- Conducting authorized business activities
- Professional development related to job duties
- Reasonable personal use that does not interfere with work
- Communication and collaboration with colleagues

### 4.2 User Responsibilities

All users must:
- Protect their login credentials and never share them
- Lock workstations when unattended
- Report security incidents immediately
- Follow data classification and handling requirements
- Complete required security training
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable multi-factor authentication where available

### 4.3 Prohibited Activities

Users must NOT:
- Share credentials or allow unauthorized access
- Access systems or data without authorization
- Install unauthorized software on company devices
- Bypass or disable security controls
- Use company resources for illegal activities
- Harass, threaten, or discriminate against others
- Download or distribute pirated software or content
- Use resources for personal financial gain
- Intentionally introduce malware or viruses
- Attempt to hack or probe systems
- Misrepresent identity in communications

## 5. System and Network Use

### 5.1 Internet Use

**Acceptable:**
- Research and reference for work purposes
- Professional networking (LinkedIn, industry forums)
- News and current events (reasonable use)
- Personal use during breaks (limited)

**Prohibited:**
- Accessing pornographic, violent, or illegal content
- Downloading unauthorized software or media
- Using peer-to-peer file sharing for personal content
- Circumventing web filters or proxy restrictions
- Excessive personal use that impacts productivity

### 5.2 Email Use

**Acceptable:**
- Business communications
- Reasonable personal messages
- Professional networking

**Prohibited:**
- Sending spam or chain letters
- Sending confidential data without encryption
- Forwarding work emails to personal accounts
- Opening suspicious attachments or links
- Impersonating others

**Email Security:**
- Verify sender identity before responding to sensitive requests
- Do not click links in unexpected emails
- Report phishing attempts to security@ilanaimmersive.com
- Use encryption for confidential information

### 5.3 Software Use

**Requirements:**
- Only install software authorized by IT
- Keep software updated with security patches
- Use only licensed software
- Remove software when no longer needed

**Prohibited:**
- Installing pirated or cracked software
- Installing software that could compromise security
- Disabling or uninstalling security software

## 6. Data Handling

### 6.1 Customer Data

**Requirements:**
- Access only customer data necessary for your job
- Never share customer data externally without authorization
- Handle customer data according to classification policy
- Report any suspected data breach immediately

**Prohibited:**
- Accessing customer data out of curiosity
- Storing customer data on personal devices
- Sharing customer data with unauthorized persons
- Retaining customer data after it's no longer needed

### 6.2 Confidential Information

**Requirements:**
- Mark confidential documents appropriately
- Share only with authorized recipients
- Use secure methods for transmission
- Dispose of properly when no longer needed

### 6.3 Personal Data

- Do not collect personal data unless required for business
- Store personal data securely
- Delete personal data when no longer needed
- Honor data subject requests per privacy policy

## 7. Mobile and Remote Work

### 7.1 Mobile Device Use

**Company Devices:**
- Use for authorized business purposes
- Keep device physically secure
- Enable screen lock with PIN/biometric
- Report lost/stolen devices immediately
- Allow remote wipe capability

**Personal Devices (BYOD):**
- Separate personal and business data where possible
- Keep device and apps updated
- Use strong authentication
- Do not store unencrypted business data
- Allow remote wipe of business data

### 7.2 Remote Work Security

**Requirements:**
- Use secure Wi-Fi (not public networks without VPN)
- Keep work conversations private (no shoulder surfing)
- Lock devices when stepping away
- Secure physical documents
- Use company-approved tools for collaboration

## 8. Social Media

### 8.1 Personal Social Media

**Guidelines:**
- Make clear that personal views are your own
- Do not disclose confidential company information
- Be respectful in discussions about the company
- Do not share customer information or experiences

### 8.2 Official Social Media

**Requirements:**
- Only authorized personnel post on behalf of company
- Follow brand guidelines and messaging
- Get approval before responding to customer issues
- Report inappropriate mentions or security concerns

## 9. Intellectual Property

### 9.1 Company Property

- All work product created for Ilana is company property
- Source code, designs, and documentation belong to Ilana
- Do not use company IP for personal projects
- Return all materials upon departure

### 9.2 Third-Party Property

- Respect copyrights and licenses
- Do not use unlicensed software or content
- Properly attribute open-source software
- Follow terms of service for third-party tools

## 10. Monitoring

### 10.1 Company Rights

Ilana Immersive reserves the right to:
- Monitor use of company systems and networks
- Access company email and files
- Review access logs and audit trails
- Inspect company devices

### 10.2 User Acknowledgment

By using company systems, users acknowledge:
- No expectation of privacy on company systems
- Communications may be monitored
- Violations may result in disciplinary action
- Illegal activity may be reported to authorities

## 11. Security Incident Reporting

### 11.1 What to Report

Report immediately:
- Lost or stolen devices
- Suspected phishing or social engineering
- Unauthorized access attempts
- Malware or virus detection
- Suspicious system behavior
- Unintended data disclosure
- Security policy violations

### 11.2 How to Report

**Primary Contact:**
- Email: security@ilanaimmersive.com
- Include: What happened, when, any relevant details

**If Urgent:**
- Contact CEO directly
- Do not delay reporting while gathering details

## 12. Compliance

### 12.1 Training

- All users complete security awareness training at onboarding
- Annual refresher training is required
- Role-specific training for sensitive positions
- Training completion is tracked

### 12.2 Enforcement

**Violations may result in:**
- Verbal or written warning
- Additional training requirements
- Loss of system access privileges
- Suspension or termination
- Legal action (for illegal activities)

**Severity Factors:**
- Intentional vs. accidental
- First offense vs. repeat
- Impact on company or customers
- Cooperation with investigation

### 12.3 Exceptions

- Exceptions require written approval from executive leadership
- Exceptions are time-limited and reviewed
- Compensating controls may be required
